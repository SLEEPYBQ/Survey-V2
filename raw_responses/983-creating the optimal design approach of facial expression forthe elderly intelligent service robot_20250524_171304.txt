## needs_and_expectations
Answer: Elderly users prefer expression designs that are warm, friendly, simple, easy to understand, clear, eye-catching, and evoke emotional resonance.
Source: The majority of participants (41) felt that the emoji design needed to be warm and friendly so as to bring a sense of approachability and comfort. The majority of participants (26) expected that the design of expressions for older users should be simple and easy to understand so as not to cause unnecessary confusion.
