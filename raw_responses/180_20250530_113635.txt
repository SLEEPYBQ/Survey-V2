## key_findings
Answer: More than half of Swedish municipalities use pet robots for older adults, primarily for dementia care to lower stress and increase safety.
Source: "The result showed that more than half of the municipalities use pet robots for older adults, and most commonly for people with dementia to lower stress and increase the feeling of safety."

## additional_info
Answer: Dominance of affordable pet robots (e.g., Joy for All, JustoCat) over expensive models like Paro; need for further research on motivation behind purchases.
Source: "An important detail regarding these robots is the price picture. The Joy for All models cost approximately €100, while the JustoCat costs approximately €1000, and both are therefore significantly cheaper than other animal robots such as the seal robot Paro (close to €5200)."
