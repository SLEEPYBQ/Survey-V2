## key_findings  
Answer: Ethical challenges include privacy, data protection, safety, isolation, loss of autonomy, overstressing users, and deception. Ethical principles of nonmaleficence, beneficence, autonomy, and fairness should be applied to robotics.  
Source: "The most important questions are those of privacy and data protection, safety and responsibility as well as involvement of vulnerable persons and deception."  

## additional_info  
Answer: The paper discusses ethical guidelines for deploying robots, including informed consent, privacy, safety, and addressing vulnerability.
